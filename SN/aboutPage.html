    ABOUT

    SEAFOOD NAKAMA?

    Overlooking on the SCBD Jakarta sits a cozy Seafood restaurant. For over 1 years, Seafood Nakama has been serving up fresh dishes, like seafood, house made sauce & home style cooking, as well as delicious variations on the classics.


    The idea for Seafood Nakama started because the founders had a mission to spread their love for Indonesian seafood as a local brand from Indonesia throughout the world.


    We start operations in 2022.
